Arroz 3 Delicias

Our version of the classic Three Delights Rice is an ode to the fusion of flavors and textures. Juicy pieces of chicken, shrimp, and fresh vegetables are masterfully combined with steamed rice, creating a dish that will delight lovers of Asian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • Steamed rice
  • Chicken
  • Shrimp
  • Fresh vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
