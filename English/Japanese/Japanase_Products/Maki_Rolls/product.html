Maki Rolls

Indulge your senses in the exquisite world of Maki Rolls, a true masterpiece in the realm of Japanese cuisine. These hand-rolled sushi delights are crafted with precision, combining fresh ingredients and traditional techniques to create a symphony of flavors that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh sushi rice
  • Nori seaweed
  • Sashimi-grade fish (e.g., tuna, salmon)
  • Vegetables (e.g., cucumber, avocado)
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger for serving
