Sushi Nigiri

Embark on a culinary journey where simplicity meets sophistication with our exquisite Sushi Nigiri. These bite-sized marvels are a testament to the art of sushi, capturing the essence of Japanese culinary finesse. Our expert chefs meticulously select premium ingredients, ensuring each piece is a harmonious blend of texture and flavor. Whether it's the buttery richness of Bluefin Tuna, the subtle sweetness of shrimp, or the umami goodness of salmon.

Ingredients:

  • Sashimi-grade fish (e.g., Bluefin Tuna, shrimp, salmon)
  • Sushi rice
  • Nori seaweed
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger for serving
